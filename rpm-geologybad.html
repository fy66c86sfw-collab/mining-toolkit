Northern Ontario - Archean Belt

Rock Type: Hard metamorphic (Greenstone belt)

Depth: 500-2000m

Drilling: Hard rock drilling, 180-220 bar percussion, 80-120 RPM

Deposits: Gold, nickel, copper

Manitoba - Thompson Belt

Rock Type: Mixed metamorphic (granites, metasediments)

Depth: 400-1500m

Drilling: Medium-hard rock, 150-180 bar, 100-150 RPM

Deposits: Nickel, copper, gold

British Columbia - Coast Range

Rock Type: Variable (granitic to metamorphic)

Depth: 200-1000m

Drilling: Medium rock, 150-170 bar, 120-160 RPM

Deposits: Copper, gold, molybdenum

Quebec - Superior Province

Rock Type: Hard greenstone belts

Depth: 300-2000m

Drilling: Hard rock, 180-220 bar, 80-120 RPM

Deposits: Gold, copper, zinc

Rock Type Effects on Drilling

  • Hard Rock: High percussion, low RPM. Larger flake cuttings.
  • Medium Rock: Balanced settings. Chip-sized cuttings.
  • Soft Rock: Lower percussion, higher RPM. Fine chip cuttings.
