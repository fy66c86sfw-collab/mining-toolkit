About Mining Toolkit

Mining Toolkit helps you quickly calculate drilling parameters, bit sizes, penetration rates, and other essential metrics used in mining operations and geological work. Built with practical field experience in mind, this app focuses on Canadian mining contexts and regional geology considerations.

Features

Drilling & Reaming Calculations
Calculate optimal RPM and speeds for various drill bit diameters and rock types.
Equipment Reference
Quick access to drill specifications, bit parameters, and machinery data.
Geological Data
Regional rock hardness and penetration rate reference for Canadian mining areas.
Offline Access
All calculations and data available without internet connection.
